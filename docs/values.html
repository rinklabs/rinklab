Our Values

This tool is built by volunteers, for volunteers. We believe coaching resources should be accessible. All we ask is that this tool remains non-profit: no fees for yourself, your team, or your league. Our goal is simple: We want kids to learn, improve, and continue playing hockey!

Our Motivation

Playing house league hockey has taught us so much. How to support your teammates, how to respect your oppounents. How to behave when you win. How to behave when you lose. It's also given us lasting friendships, and safe places. We're parents, we're neighbours, and we're coaches to young peoples learning how to be adults.

We know the challenge of creating a practice plan. Having grown up in house leagues ourselves, we understand the rink is more than just ice. We built this lab to ensure every coach has the resources to create a high-quality, collaborative environment without the hurdle of expensive software.

In house leagues, practices. We focus on three core pillars for every session:

  • Relentless Effort: Compete on Every Puck. We value perfect effort over perfect performance.
  • Active Learning: Coachability. Listen with intent and engage with the game.
  • Team First: Support your teammates on the ice and in the locker room.

We hope this makes your coaching journey more rewarding for every players and coaches. See you at the rink!
