Who We Are

We are a group of volunteers passionate about making hockey coaching resources accessible to everyone. We started coaching when our children were young, and quickly realized how hard it was to find good, free tools to help us plan practices. Our kids played house league, so practices were often short and infrequent, making it even more important to make the most of our time on the ice. With our busy work and home lives, we wanted a tool that could help us create an engaging and fruitful practice and share amongst our fellow coaches. Also, we were always on the lookout for drills, and we were more than happy to share them. Afterall, an opponent one year could be a teammate next.

There are some great tools out there, but we found most of them either gave limited time access or limited number of drills for the free tiers. Also, these tools had features meant for more compentitive levels of hockey that were uneeded for house league coaches. We wanted something free that any coach could use without a limited learning curve. We built Rink Lab to fill that gap, and to create a collaborative space for coaches to share ideas and resources.

Our Values

This tool is built by volunteers, for volunteers. We believe coaching resources should be accessible. All we ask is that this tool remains non-profit: no fees for yourself, your team, or your league. Our goal is simple: We want kids to learn, improve, and continue playing hockey!

Our Motivation

Playing house league hockey has taught us how to support your teammates, how to respect your opponents, how to behave when you win, how to behave when you lose. It's also given us lasting friendships and safe places. We're parents, we're neighbours, and we're coaches to young people learning how to be adults.

We know the challenge of creating a constructive practice plan. We understand the rink is more than just ice, its a place for families and communities. We built this lab to ensure every coach has access to a high-quality, collaborative environment without the hurdle of expensive software.

Our Core Coaching Values

We believe in fostering a positive, inclusive, and growth-oriented environment for players of all skill levels. Our core coaching values are designed to promote teamwork, respect, and a love for the game. We encourage coaches to focus on effort, learning, and putting the team first in every practice and game.

  • Relentless Effort: Compete on Every Puck. We value perfect effort over perfect performance.
  • Active Learning: Coachability. Listen with intent and engage with the game.
  • Team First: Support your teammates on the ice and in the locker room.

A Final Note

We are committed to keeping Rink Lab free and accessible for all coaches. If you share our values and want to support our mission, please consider contributing your time, resources, or expertise. Together, we can create a vibrant community of coaches dedicated to fostering a love for hockey and helping players grow both on and off the ice. If you are interested in volunteering, or have questions about the Lab, please contact rinklabsadmin[at]gmail.com.
