HOCKEY DRILL LABS

Coaching & Practices, Simplified.

Volunteer coaching is a challenge; building an intuitive practice plan shouldn't be. Our mission is to provide amateur youth hockey mentors with free, professional-grade tools to design drills, collaborate with peers, and lead with confidence. High impact, lightweight, and always open-source.

Drills Lab Canvas

Design custom drills using high-fidelity rink templates. Place players, draw complex patterns, and export clear diagrams for your team.

Practice Lab

Create a structured 60-minute practice plan tailored to your team's development goals using the practices you and your fellow coaches designed.

Our Values

This tool is built by volunteers, for volunteers. We believe coaching resources should be accessible. All we ask is that this tool remains non-profit: no fees for yourself, your team, or your league. Our goal is simple: We want kids to learn, improve, and continue playing hockey!

Our Motivation

Playing house league hockey has taught us so much. How to support your teammates, how to respect your oppounents. How to behave when you win. How to behave when you lose. It's also given us lasting friendships, and safe places. We're parents, we're neighbours, and we're coaches to young peoples learning how to be adults.

Developing a practice can be tough, especially if you're new to coaching. There are lots of products on the market, and we are not trying to compete with them. We wanted to give coaches a way to easily put a practice together that is free, intuitive, and collaborative. We hope you enjoy the product.

In house leagues, practices. We focus on three core pillars for every session:

  • Relentless Effort: Give your best every single shift.
  • Active Learning: Listen and engage with your coaches
  • Team First: Support your teammates on and off the ice.

We hope this makes your coaching journey more rewarding, translating into a better experience for every young player on your roster.
