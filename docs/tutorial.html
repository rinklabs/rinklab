USER GUIDE

Mastering the Drill & Practice Labs

Registration

Registering is necessary for saving your work and accessing all features of the labs.


1

Create Your Username

Use the Create account on the home page to create a new coach profile. The profile can be used to save drills and practice plans.

2

Sign In

Use the Sign In on the home page to sign in to your existing coach profile. Once signed in your username will be displayed in the top right corner in the canvas and practice labs and you will be able to save your work.

Drills Lab Canvas

1

Set Your Rink

Use the ◑ Half Rink toggle in the right sidebar to switch between full-ice and zone-specific drills.

2

Draw & Place

Select tools and icons from the right toolbar. Use the Select (⌖) tool to move icons, or use the Line and Path tools to map out player movement and puck flow.

3

Describe & Tag

Write the drill descriptions and use the TAGS to categorize drills and search for them later.

4

Save Your Work

Press Ctrl + S (or Cmd + S) to save to the cloud immediately. Use JSON ↓ to download a backup of your drill file.

4

Delete Drills

Open the 📂 Library, find your drill, and click the Delete button. Confirm the popup to permanently remove it.

Practice Lab

1

Build Your Drill Sequence

Search for saved drills in the sidebar and click Add to move them into your 60-minute timeline. Use the Quick Drill to add drills without pictures.

2

Adjust Timing

Update the duration for each drill. The generator will track your total practice time to ensure you stay within your ice slot.

2

Checking Timing

Look at the Timeline Bar at the bottom. The Total Time shows how many minutes you've used out of your 60-minute slot. The bar will turn orange if you exceed your ice time.

3

Share with Coaches

Use the Share button to generate a link or export a summary for your assistant coaches and players.

4

Export to PDF

Use the PDF ↓ button to download your practice as a PDF, ready to be used on-ice!

Advanced Canvas Styling

1

Player Labels

When a player is selected, use the properties panel on the bottom right to change their label (e.g., "D1", "F2"). Use the size slider to adjust visibility.

2

Changing Colour

Select any object and use the Stroke or Fill colour pickers in the toolbar to change its appearance.

3

Coaches Symbol

Select a player (letter) and check the Circle option to display a coach symbol, which is a letter with a circle around it.
